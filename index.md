## About Me

I am a **Professor at Chongqing University**, working in the field of **polarimetric SAR image understanding and interpretation**.  
My research focuses on **SAR target detection and recognition**, **polarimetric theory**, and **machine learning-based remote sensing**.  
I am particularly interested in combining **SAR imaging geometry**, **electromagnetic scattering theory**, and **deep neural networks** for intelligent SAR data analysis.
